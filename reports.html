2. Stanford Center for Health Education

The Stanford Center for Health Education offers structured online programs that are more directly related to health topics.

These include:

Professional Education Programs

  • Self-paced online courses designed to prepare learners in health and wellness fields
  • Earn a certificate upon completion
  • Examples can include topics like Machine Learning in Medicine or Medical Teaching
  • Content is designed and delivered by Stanford Medicine experts.

Graduate Health Education

  • Online graduate-level courses in topics like epidemiology, clinical research design, and biostatistics
  • Some courses earn academic credit or a graduate certificate (more advanced than typical short online courses).

3. Stanford Medicine Continuing Medical Education (CME)

Stanford School of Medicine through its Continuing Medical Education program offers:

  • A large catalog of online, on-demand medical education modules
  • Webinars, recorded lectures, and podcasts covering clinical specialties such as genetics, cardiology, infectious disease, nutrition, dermatology, AI in healthcare, and more
  • Completion can lead to continuing medical education credits (CME) for health professionals
  • Non-professionals can often access the same content and receive a certificate of completion (without CME credit).

Stanford CME platform: https://stanford.cloud-cme.com/

Examples of topics you might find:

  • Genetics & Genomics
  • Nutrition topics
